## Preregister for beta

You can sign-up to help us out during our beta release.  This is a great way to see the app in action, as well as help us work
out any kinks before we release it to the public.

1. Enter in your email address to sign-up for the beta [here](https://rallybehindyou.com)
2. We will send you an email to fill out a pre beta registration from

That's it.  You will then have access to the suite of tools.
